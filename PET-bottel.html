Plastic Bottle (PET) Recyclable

Commonly used for beverages, this type of plastic is widely recycled. Please rinse, empty, and compress before placing in the recycling bin.

Key Info

Category: Plastic

Type: PET

Common Use: Water bottles, Juice bottles

How to Recycle

1 Rinse the bottle
2 Remove the cap
3 Let it dry
4 Place in plastic recycling bin

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • ❌ Do not crush bottle with liquid inside
  • ❌ Do not mix with food waste
  • ❌ Do not leave cap attached

Recycling Center CTA

Find nearby recycling centers that accept this material

Additional Info

What happens after recycling?

PET bottles are sorted, cleaned, and shredded into flakes. These flakes are melted down and reshaped into new products like clothing fibers, carpet, or new plastic containers.

Environmental impact

Recycling a single plastic bottle can save enough energy to power a 60-watt light bulb for up to 6 hours.

Alternative reuse ideas

Before recycling, consider reusing bottles for DIY crafts, simple planters for seedlings, or as watering cans.
