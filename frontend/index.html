Quick Recycling Tips

✅ Clean and dry all containers before tossing them in the bin.
✅ Never bag your recyclables; keep them loose in the bin.
✅ Flatten cardboard boxes to save space for other materials.
✅ Keep hazardous materials like paint and chemicals out of standard bins.
✅ When in doubt, throw it out to avoid contaminating the batch.
✅ Leave caps on plastic bottles, but remove them from glass jars.
